Undergraduate study

Sorbonne University Abu Dhabi offers eleven undergraduate bachelor programmes, delivered by permanent faculty based in Abu Dhabi and Visiting professors. All programmes follow the academic curricula defined in Paris, except for the bachelors specific to the Abu Dhabi campus. However, all programmes offer high quality education and answer the European academic standards. Graduates receive a Parisian bachelor degree issued by Sorbonne University in the fields of Humanities and Sciences and by Université Paris Cité in the fields of Law and Economics.

    Sorbonne University Abu Dhabi's Campus Life

    Sorbonne Abu Dhabi is centrally located on Al Reem Island on a state-of-the-art campus. Offering students a world-class education within a culturally diverse environment, the university continues to make a remarkable impact on higher education for students from all over the world.

    At the heart of the UAE is Abu Dhabi, the country’s capital and cultural hub. Offering students a unique blend of tradition and modernity, Abu Dhabi is one of the World’s fastest growing economies and is continually diversifying. This has widened Abu Dhabi’s appeal for students and graduates as dynamic career opportunities across many different industries become more widely available.
